EXAMPLE COMPANY - DATA PRIVACY POLICY
=======================================

This is an EXAMPLE policy document. Replace with your actual organizational policies.

1. PURPOSE
----------
This policy establishes guidelines for the collection, use, storage, and sharing of customer and employee data.

2. SCOPE
--------
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who have access to company data.

3. DATA CLASSIFICATION
---------------------
3.1 Public Data: Information that can be freely shared
3.2 Internal Data: Information for internal use only
3.3 Confidential Data: Sensitive information requiring protection
3.4 Restricted Data: Highly sensitive data (PII, financial, health)

4. DATA COLLECTION
------------------
4.1 Only collect data necessary for business purposes
4.2 Obtain consent before collecting personal information
4.3 Clearly communicate data collection purposes to data subjects

5. DATA USAGE
-------------
5.1 Use data only for stated purposes
5.2 Do not use customer data for unauthorized marketing
5.3 Employees must not access data outside their job responsibilities

6. DATA SHARING
---------------
6.1 Customer data must NOT be shared with third parties without explicit consent
6.2 Data sharing with vendors requires:
    - Written data processing agreement
    - Privacy impact assessment
    - Manager approval
6.3 Bulk data exports require approval from Data Protection Officer

7. DATA STORAGE
---------------
7.1 Store data in approved systems only
7.2 Encrypt sensitive data at rest and in transit
7.3 Implement appropriate access controls

8. DATA RETENTION
-----------------
8.1 Retain data only as long as necessary
8.2 Follow retention schedules:
    - Customer transaction data: 7 years
    - Marketing data: 3 years
    - Application logs: 90 days

9. DATA DISPOSAL
----------------
9.1 Securely delete data when no longer needed
9.2 Use approved data destruction methods
9.3 Document all data disposal activities

10. INCIDENT RESPONSE
--------------------
10.1 Report suspected data breaches immediately
10.2 Follow incident response procedures
10.3 Notify affected parties within 72 hours if required

11. VIOLATIONS
-------------
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

12. POLICY UPDATES
-----------------
This policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed.
