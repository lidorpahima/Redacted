EXAMPLE COMPANY - HUMAN RESOURCES POLICY
=========================================

This is an EXAMPLE policy document. Replace with your actual organizational policies.

1. TIME OFF POLICY
------------------
1.1 Vacation Days
    - Full-time employees: 15 days per year
    - Maximum consecutive vacation: 3 weeks
    - Must be approved by manager 2 weeks in advance

1.2 Sick Leave
    - 10 days per year
    - No doctor's note required for absences < 3 days
    - Contact manager before 9 AM on day of absence

1.3 Personal Days
    - 3 days per year
    - Can be used for any reason
    - Same approval process as vacation

2. REMOTE WORK POLICY
---------------------
2.1 Eligibility
    - Employees with 6+ months tenure
    - Role must be suitable for remote work
    - Manager approval required

2.2 Schedule
    - Hybrid employees: minimum 2 days in office per week
    - Fully remote: attend monthly team meetings in person
    - Core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM (your local timezone)

2.3 Equipment
    - Company provides laptop and monitor
    - Home internet required (minimum 25 Mbps)
    - IT support available for company equipment only

3. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
---------------------------
3.1 Employee information is strictly confidential
3.2 Do not share:
    - Salaries or compensation details
    - Performance reviews
    - Medical information
    - Disciplinary actions

3.3 Violations may result in termination

4. WORKPLACE CONDUCT
--------------------
4.1 Professional behavior expected at all times
4.2 Zero tolerance for:
    - Discrimination or harassment
    - Violence or threats
    - Substance abuse

5. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
----------------------
5.1 Annual reviews conducted each January
5.2 Mid-year check-ins in July
5.3 Feedback should be documented and shared with employee

6. TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
---------------------------
6.1 $1,500 annual training budget per employee
6.2 Required trainings:
    - Security awareness (annual)
    - Harassment prevention (annual)
    - Role-specific compliance

7. EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
--------------------
7.1 Health insurance: starts on first day
7.2 401(k): eligible after 3 months
7.3 Life insurance: 1x annual salary

8. TERMINATION
--------------
8.1 At-will employment
8.2 Notice period:
    - Employee resignation: 2 weeks
    - Company termination: 2 weeks severance (in most cases)
